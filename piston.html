When I joined the MIT Rocket Team in my freshman year, the team had just competed in IREC 2017. We launched two rockets, Raziel and Virgo. Raziel had a target altitude of 10,000 feet and Virgo had a target altitude of 30,000 feet. Both rockets used a compressedcold gas canister to separate and deploy the drogue. A black powder charge would be used to deploy the main. Although Raziel won second place in its category for IREC, both rockets' recovery systems failed.
